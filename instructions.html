Instructions

Use arrow keys to navigate.

Each block can only be accessed once. Each unaccessed area will be marked with a black tile, and each accessed area will be marked with a gray, purple, green, or blue tile after you have moved from the block.

gray = normal tile
purple = poison tile
green = antidote tile
blue = riddle tile

Each level has a certain number of poison, antidote, and riddle blocks.
If you step on a poison block, you lose a life.
If you step on an antidote block, you gain a life.
If you step on a riddle block, you get a riddle to answer. Answer correctly, and you'll get another life.

Note that all blocks must be stepped on exactly once to proveed to the next level, so plan your lives carefully
